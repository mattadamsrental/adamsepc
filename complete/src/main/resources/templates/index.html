What is an EPC?

An Energy Performance Certificate (EPC) contains information about how energy is used in a home or a business premises, along with details of how much the energy used actually costs.

An EPC gives a property an energy efficiency rating from A (most efficient) to G (least efficient) and is valid for 10 years.

Energy Performance Certificates

Energy Performance Certificates (EPCs) are needed whenever a property in Daventry is:

  • built
  • sold
  • rented

You must order an EPC for potential buyers and tenants before you market your Daventry property to sell or rent.

An EPC contains:

  • information about your Daventry property’s energy use and typical energy costs
  • recommendations about how to reduce energy use and save money
  • An EPC gives your Daventry property an energy efficiency rating from A (most efficient) to G (least efficient) and is valid for 10 years.
