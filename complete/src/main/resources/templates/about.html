About Us

Adams EPC provide energy performance certificates in Northants and surrounding areas (check our other sites for full coverage details).

Our locally bases energy assessor is fully accredited by NHER-SAVA and Stroma and has undergone CRB checks to give you confidence and peace of mind.

With over 6 years experience in the field and over 6000 assessments completed, you can be sure that you can trust us to provide a no hassle and efficient local service.

To book your appointment, or if you want us to call you back to discuss further details, please use our contact form. Alternatively you could give us a call on (07769) 696777 and we will happy to discuss your specific requirements.

We can often provide same day or short notice appointments at your convenience and you can pay on the day.
